Problem Statement

To find the arbitrarily shaped clusters incremental in nature with dynamically growing datasets exhibiting the increment of the dataset not just with respect to class (Class-Incremental) but also in the distribution of each class. The clusters thus formed should lead to train a classifier for detection of the classes present in crowd sourced data.

Introduction

Incremental Learning (IL) is a method of machine learning in which input data is continuously used to extend the existing model's knowledge i.e. to further train the model. Incremental learning plays a key role in many real-world applications where data arrives over time. It is mainly concerned with learning models in an ever-changing environment. Training large networks to accommodate new, previously unseen data demands high computational time and energy requirements.
